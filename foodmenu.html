Weekend Carvery

One of our main features will be the Saturday/Sunday Roast Carvery Brunch, where the customers can experience the ultimate Weekend Carvery at the Queen's Head. Our succulent roasted meats, perfectly cooked vegetables, and traditional Yorkshire puddings are a feast for the senses. Join us every Saturday and Sunday for a dining experience that brings the best of British cuisine to Dubai Green Community. This will be operated on a buffet-style basis where our chef will serve the cut of your meat selection. The vast selection of sides, including the kids' favourites, will be on a help-yourself, all-you-can-eat basis.

Our carvery includes a selection of Starters:

  • Soup Selections
  • Various Salads
  • Choice of Breads

Selection of Mains to Include:

  • Roast Turkey
  • Roast Pork with Crispy Crackling
  • Roast Chicken
  • Vegetarian Options
